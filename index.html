About Me

I’m John Escolano, a passionate creator with a diverse background that connects hospitality, music, and technology. I spent 15 years in Hong Kong, where I refined my communication skills, creativity, and adaptability while working in dynamic bars and restaurants as a bartender and performing as a DJ. These experiences taught me how to engage with people, build meaningful connections, and bring energy into everything I do.

When I moved to Canada in 2018, I entered a new chapter of growth. I worked in various roles while adapting to a new country and building new skills. During this time, I completed a Network and IT program, which helped me step into the tech industry. I began working as a Desktop Support Technician.

Today, I work at a logistics company, continuing to grow my technical abilities and learning more about modern systems and workflow optimization. Alongside my career, I am expanding my passion for software development by building modern, user-focused web projects.

Welcome to my space — where creativity meets technology, and every project reflects the unique blend of experience, artistry, and innovation that defines who I am.
